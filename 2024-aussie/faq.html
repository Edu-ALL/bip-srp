Program Overview

ALL-in Global Immersion Program is a university and career immersion program in the heart of Melbourne and Sydney city for students who are interested in pursuing higher education and career journey in Australia. We provide real-life implementation in the business, science, tech, and design fields through hands-on workshops, company and university visits, and networking sessions.

This program gives you a teaser of the exciting opportunities you can explore as a student in Australia, and even beyond graduation as you build your career!

Through this program, we want you to broaden your horizons and take action, connect with like-minded students, and confirm the career outlook and university major that you want to pursue in Australia.

Students need direct exposure to multiple streams of a career as they explore their interests and passions.

By combining fun excursions, multidisciplinary workshops, and insightful company visits, students will discover their potential further, get exposed to various in-demand areas, and be equipped with the steps and pathways to a successful career.

With personal engagement towards learning, they will grow inherent motivation to learn and develop both technically, mentally, and socially.

This year, the Global Immersion Program will cover four fields: Business, Science, Tech, and Design.

The ideal candidate for this program is a middle or high school student from Grade 9 to 12 who is driven, and considering studying in Australia and pursuing job opportunities there.

Any previous knowledge in business, science, technology, or design is not required.

Global Immersion Program is a 10-day program that runs from June 24th to July 3rd, 2024.

The application is open from January to the end of June 2024 on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Fee and Scholarship

This program costs 3,750 USD.

The fee includes the cost for accommodation + breakfast, visa fee, travel insurance, ground transport (with a variety of coaches and public transport), meals (18x), all program entrance fees, and certificate.

This fee excludes flight tickets, -souvenirs and personal shopping, and extra meals on free days.

The program fee does not include the flight ticket, but we can help with the flight arrangement along with other participants.

Unfortunately, we are not opening scholarship opportunities for this program. However, you can earn a special fee with our offers. Fill in the form and ask our team for available offers!
