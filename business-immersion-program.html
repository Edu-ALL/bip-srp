Incubate Your Multi-Million-Dollar Startup Ideas!

ALL-in Business Immersion Program is a 5-day program designed for high school students who are interested in business subjects. Professional services, from investment banking to digital marketing and various industries from retail, e-commerce, to early-stage startup will be covered here.

Through a mix of hands-on workshops, company visits, and networking lunches and dinners, students will be exposed to real business dealings and develop business acumen. At the end of the program, students will pitch their ideas and prototypes to angel investors from various backgrounds.

WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

  1. Four-days hands-on workshop at Reactor School focusing on new venture creation cycle. Students will pick up technical skills and soft skills, by experiencing what it means to be a co-founder and pick up start-up best practices such as user-centric design, lean methodology, validation and value creation.
  2. Visits to global companies, such as UOB; Innovate 360; JTC Launchpad; and many more.
  3. Attend sharing sessions and networking lunches and dinners with company management teams.
  4. Campus tours to Singapore’s top universities.

*Itinerary is subject to change. Any changes will be made without prior notification to the public.

At the end of the program, students will deliver an abridged investor pitch deck with an accompanying prototype, centered around a selected theme. Parents are ENCOURAGED to attend this presentation.

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN THIS PROGRAM
  1. Develop entrepreneurial skills and network with industry professionals.
  2. Build your own product prototype, get a chance to get funded and become a startup founder.
  3. Explore your intellectual interests and career options.
  4. Polish your CV with proof of certificates & student awards.
  5. Connect and network with like-minded peers and make new friends globally.
