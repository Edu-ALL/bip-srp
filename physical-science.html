ALL-in Science Research Immersion Program: Physical Science is a 6-day trip program that brings students to experience hands-on lab work using cutting-edge equipment not available in school and witness the forefront of technologies, culminating in presenting their first scientific abstract in a mini symposium.

Through a mix of research, workshops, and corporate and lab visits and networking, students will get exposed to the applications of technology in the fields of robotics, Artificial Intelligent, computer sciences, renewable energy, environmental sustainability.

WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

  1. Hands-on workshop and laboratorium work at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), National University of Singapore (NUS), Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD).
  2. Visits to global science and medical centres, such as Tampines Park, Science Centres.
  3. Attend sharing sessions and networking lunches and dinners with professors and undergraduate students.
  4. Campus tours to Singapore’s top universities.

At the end of the program, students will present a scientific abstract in a mini symposium. Parents are ENCOURAGED to attend this presentation.

*Itinerary is subject to change. Any changes will be made without prior notification to the public.

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN THIS PROGRAM

  1. Develop research skills and network with industry professionals.
  2. Formulate your own research questions and get ready for independent research work.
  3. Explore your intellectual interests and career options.
  4. Polish your CV with proof of certificates & student awards.
  5. Connect and network with like-minded peers and make new friends globally.
