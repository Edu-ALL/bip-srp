GLOBAL IMMERSION PROGRAM 2023 SCHOLARSHIP

If you are interested in joining the Global Immersion Program but need help in funding the program fee, you are welcome to apply for the scholarship. We offer a full-ride, need-based scholarship to both Business and Science Research Program.

Criteria and Eligibility

  1. Demonstrate financial needs (see below).
  2. Have a rigorous school curriculum.
  3. Overall average grades >80.
  4. Currently taking relevant academic subjects (e.g. business for BIP or Chemistry for SRIP).
  5. Be able to show evidence of your interest in subjects/topics covered in the program.
  6. Has a vision about future academic and/or career plans.

Application Requirements

  1. Resume
    Please upload your CV to the designated question in the Application Form.
  2. Essay
    The application essay is to help you explain about who you are and how this program would benefit you in achieving your goals. Please answer the prompts below in ONE essay (500-650 words):
    • What are your academic interests and career goals?
    • What have you done to pursue such interests and goals?
    • Why do you want to join this program and how will it help you achieve your goals?
    • Why do you deserve this scholarship?
  3. Letter of Recommendation
    Send a letter from your teachers/mentor that explains why this program is important for the student and why he/she needs the scholarship.
  4. Financial proof
    Please submit your parents’ bank statement in the last 3 months.
