ALL-in Innovators-in-Residence (Junior Program) is a 6-day trip program that brings students to experience and learn in a fun and hands-on way, working on creative projects, and collaborating with industry professionals and academic scholars to solve real-world problems.

Through a mix of hands-on workshops, company visits, and networking lunches and dinners, students will be exposed to real business dealings and develop business acumen. At the end of the program, students will pitch their ideas and prototypes to angel investors from various backgrounds.

IN THIS PROGRAM, YOU WILL…

Dive into creative thinking and collaborate to develop UN's SDG-focused projects

Participate in technical hands-on workshops covering STEM+arts

Visit cutting-edge labs, learning facilities, and top universities in Singapore

Engage in networking events with industry professionals

Project Choices

Wearables and e-textiles to aid human bodily functions

Detection program as a tool to measure the quality of our environment

Interactive chatbot to help people find information easily

Robot training to make it understand gestures and facial expressions

At the end of the program, students will deliver an abridged investor pitch deck with an accompanying prototype, centered around a selected theme

Parents are ENCOURAGED to attend this presentation

*Itinerary is subject to change. Any changes will be made without prior notification to the public
