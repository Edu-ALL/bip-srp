The Scientific Approach to Solve the World’s Most Pressing Problems

ALL-in Science Research Immersion Program is a 5-day bootcamp that brings students to experience hands-on lab work using cutting-edge equipment not available in school and witness the forefront of technologies, culminating in presenting their first scientific abstract in a mini symposium.

Through a mix of research, workshops, and corporate and lab visits and networking; students will get exposed to the applications of technology in the fields of genetic engineering, renewable energy, medicine, systems engineering, robotics, and AI. At the end of the program, students will present their findings in a mini research symposium in front of university professors.

WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

  1. Four-days hands-on workshop and laboratorium work at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), National University of Singapore (NUS), Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD).
  2. Visits to global science and medical centres, such as NKF Kidney Discovery Centre; MiRobotics; and many more.
  3. Attend sharing sessions and networking lunches and dinners with professors and undergraduate students.
  4. Campus tours to Singapore’s top universities.

*Itinerary is subject to change. Any changes will be made without prior notification to the public.

At the end of the program, students will present a scientific abstract in a mini symposium. Parents are ENCOURAGED to attend this presentation.

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN THIS PROGRAM
  1. Develop research skills and network with industry professionals.
  2. Formulate your own research questions and get ready for independent research work.
  3. Explore your intellectual interests and career options.
  4. Polish your CV with proof of certificates & student awards.
  5. Connect and network with like-minded peers and make new friends globally.
PRICE

1,950 USD

Include: Research programs and activities, accommodation, meal (breakfast), transportation.

Exclude: Flight ticket*, meal allowance (lunch and dinner), other extra expenses during stay in Singapore, entrance fees to tourist areas, visa arrangement (if you plan to stay more than 30 days in Singapore).
