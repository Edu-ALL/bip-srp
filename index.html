Want to Take It to the Next Level?

Prepare for the Entrepreneurship or Science Research Mentoring Program!

A 1-on-1 coaching with ALL-in Eduspace expert mentors and industry or research professionals, where you can turn your business pitch or simple research findings into a solid output.

What does it mean to be an entrepreneur? What does it take to succeed as one? As future CEOs and innovators, students are given a taste of what it truly takes to build a business from beginning to the end, from A-Z, from zero to hero, through 1:1 coaching. Students are taken on a journey of exploration into the business processes that happen behind all great businesses and startups in our world. They will have the opportunity to identify problems, formulate business solutions, build sustainable business strategies, and actually get their business up and running. At the end of this program, students will have a better understanding of not only the business world, but also their own strengths and weaknesses in pursuing their goal of becoming a future business leader.

Curriculum Sneak Peek:

  • Build business from scratch
  • Obtain feedback directly from the market and experienced business founders
  • Solve real challenges of small and medium companies

Are you ready to unleash the scientist in you? Have you ever wonder how scientists can achieve ground-breaking innovations? As an aspiring scientist and researcher, students are given the opportunity to evolve from just a science student into a real scientist, to deep dive into performing a real scientific research project, and to potentially solve the next million dollar question in STEM through 1-on-1 coaching. Students will be going on a journey to explore scientific interest, develop their research questions, formulate their hypothesis, and write research proposals. They will also be guided to develop their own Design of Experiment, perform real experimental and/or simulation work, analyze their experimental data, and actually write a research paper. At the end of this program, students will have a better understanding of not only the scientific research world, but also their demonstrate advanced work in their scientific field of interest, thus, becoming a future leader in the scientific community.

Curriculum Sneak Peek:

  • Design of experiment template
  • Solve real scientific problem
  • Able to use scientific analysis software and laboratorium equipments (if applicable)
  • Full scientific research paper
